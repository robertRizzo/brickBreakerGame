Game Instructions:

Welcome to Brick Breaker!

Controls:
- Move paddle: Right Arrow (→) and Left Arrow (←)
- Launch ball: Space Bar

Objective:
- Break all bricks without losing the ball.
- You have 3 lives. Score points by breaking bricks.

Tips:
- The ball bounces off walls and the paddle.
- Use paddle sections to control the ball:
- Left & Right (Red): Bounces left or Right
- Middle (Green): Bounces straight up

Good luck and enjoy!
